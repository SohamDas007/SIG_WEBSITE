About Us

Pioneering Excellence in Geospatial Education

Symbiosis Institute of Geoinformatics (SIG) is a pioneering institution offering leading-edge programs in Spatial Science, Geo Intelligence, Data Science, and Analytics. Committed to providing top-tier education, SIG prepares students to meet the growing demands of Geospatial technology, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Navigation, and e-governance. With a remarkable 100% campus project cum placement record, SIG offers programs such as M.Sc. (Geoinformatics), M.Sc. (Data Science and Spatial Analytics), M.Tech (Geoinformatics and Surveying Technology), and certificate courses in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing. Our curriculum is carefully crafted to cover both theoretical and practical aspects, ensuring graduates are well-equipped for success in the dynamic field of geospatial technologies.
