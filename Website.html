Courses We Offer

SIG Offers Courses Conferred Under the Symbiosis International University Spread Across Semesters

M.Sc. Geoinformatics

M.Sc.(Geoinformatics) offered by Symbiosis Institute of Geoinformatics (SIG) comprises 80 credits, spread across 4 semesters. Comprising two projects as expected , the degree is conferred under the Symbiosis International University. Geoinformatics is a specialized field necessitating expert knowledge. SIG has emerged as one of the leading MSc geoinformatics colleges in India with their commitment, quality of education and legacy as a institute.

M.Sc. Data Science and Spatial Analytics

SIG believes that the current trends of Artificial Intelligence techniques, including Machine learning and Deep learning aspects through MSc data science in India, are uniformly revamping a range of fields from finance and banking to healthcare and earth observations. So the institute provides some of the best data science and AI courses in India. SIG offers the one of the best M.Sc data science and AI course in India to students interested in the field.

M.Tech. Geoinformatics and ST

Geoinformatics is a broad term used for collection of a number of different technologies, approaches, processes that deal with spatial information. Our students are able to create original solutions to manage and process spatial information and proficiently interact with groups of experts in various fields. Students who have completed their MTech in Geoinformatics can be employed as GIS consultants, GIS managers, GIS Engineers, GIS analysts or GIS developers.
